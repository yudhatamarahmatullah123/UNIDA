LEARN ISLAMIC STUDIES AND ISLAMIZATION CONTEMPORARY SCIENCE

The Postgraduate Program of Universitas Darussalam Gontor (UNIDA) has entered its second decade in producing graduates dedicated to addressing the challenges faced by today's Muslims. Various efforts have been undertaken to achieve this goal, including enhancing both academic and non-academic services to align with the aspirations of the founder of Pondok Modern Darussalam Gontor in establishing a high-quality and meaningful Islamic University. Furthermore, the Postgraduate Program has incorporated UNIDA Gontor's vision, which focuses on developing proficiency in the Al-Quran Language, the Islamization of Science, and an Islamic Boarding School-based Education, into its development programs and quality standards.

Assoc. Prof. Dr. Mohammad Kholid Mushlih, Lc., M.A.

Director of the Postgraduate Program
